Transform Your Life Through Martial Arts

Adults of all shapes, sizes, and ages come to us and work to achieve BLACK SASH EXCELLENCE in all areas of their lives. For many, their training contributes to a feeling of control in their life — control of their HEALTH, SAFETY, MIND, and SPIRIT.

Whether you're interested in FITNESS, SELF-DEFENCE, or just LEARNING SOMETHING NEW, we're here to help you. And you don't have to be in shape to start — the adult classes are designed to help you get into shape, gradually and safely.

We teach Wong Shun-Leung Wing Chun (Ving Tsun). WSL was the direct student of Grandmaster Ip Man and took over the Kwoon after Ip Man's passing. He also trained Bruce Lee along with Ip Man before Bruce left Hong Kong to the USA.
