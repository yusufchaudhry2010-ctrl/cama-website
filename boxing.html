What is Spark Fitness Boxing?

Our boxing programme — Spark Fitness Boxing — is designed for all levels. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced competitor, our coaches will develop your technique, footwork, combinations and defensive skill.

Boxing at CAMA develops discipline, physical fitness and mental toughness. We work with both children and adults, with dedicated junior sessions for younger students.

Under the guidance of our experienced coaching team including Stefan Saleem and Karl Harcourt, you'll develop real boxing skills in a safe, supportive environment.

What You'll Learn

🥊 Proper stance & footwork
💥 Power combinations
🛡️ Defence & head movement
❤️ Cardiovascular fitness
🧠 Mental toughness
