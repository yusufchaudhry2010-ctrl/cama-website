Forge a Warrior's Body

Our Martial Fitness programme is designed to build the kind of strength, endurance and explosive power that martial artists need. Combining combat drills with functional training, every session will push your limits.

Led by experienced martial artists and certified instructors, sessions include bag work, pad work, circuit training, battle ropes, bodyweight exercises and more.

No martial arts experience is required. Whether you're training for competition, self-defence or just to get supremely fit, this programme delivers results.
