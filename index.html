About CAMA

A Legacy of
Excellence

The Chiltern Academy of Martial Arts was founded in 2018 by Sifu Mark Lane — a visionary with over 50 years of martial arts experience. Starting with zero students, CAMA has grown into a thriving community of over 200 dedicated practitioners.

We offer world-class instruction in Wing Chun Kung Fu, Tai Chi, Kids Judo, SparX Fitness Boxing and Personal Training — taught by black belt instructors with decades of real-world experience.
