Judo — The Gentle Way

Judo (柔道) — "The Gentle Way" — is one of the most widely practised martial arts in the world and an Olympic sport. Our Judo programme is led by Victor Webber, a 4th Dan black belt with over 50 years of experience who has places in the National kata championships.

Our founder, Sifu Mark Lane, began his martial arts journey in 1972 with Judo, achieving his first black belt in 1978 and competing at the BJC National Championships. Judo is deep in CAMA's DNA.

We offer classes for both juniors and adults. Judo develops discipline, physical fitness, self-confidence and the ability to defend yourself effectively on the ground.
