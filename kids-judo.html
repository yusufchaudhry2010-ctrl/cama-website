Junior Judo — The Gentle Way

🆓 First Class is FREE

Come and try a class completely free of charge. No commitment required.

Our kids judo programme caters to ages 7–15, running every Thursday during school term time at the Curzon Centre in Beaconsfield. We have 2 expert instructors, each with 50 years of experience — so you know your child is in the very best hands.

Judo teaches children far more than just throwing techniques. It builds discipline, respect, physical fitness, mental toughness and the ability to fall safely. Junior judoka also have the opportunity to compete locally and nationally.

Led by our legendary judo coach Victor Webber (4th Dan, 50+ years experience, National kata champion), and supported by Sifu Mark Lane who holds a black belt in Judo since 1978.
