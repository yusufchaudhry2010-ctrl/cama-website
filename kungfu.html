Wing Chun — The Art of Spring

Wing Chun (詠春) is one of the most effective and efficient martial arts in existence. Our programme follows the Wong Shun Leung lineage — the same lineage taught to Bruce Lee — making it one of the most authentic Wing Chun programmes in the UK.

Sifu Mark Lane trains directly under Master Clive Potter, a first-generation student of the legendary Wong Shun Leung with over 50 years experience.

You'll learn the classical forms (Siu Lim Tau, Chum Kiu, Biu Gee), wooden dummy (Mook Jong) training, the weapons (butterfly swords and long pole), and the unique sensitivity training known as Chi Sao (sticky hands).
