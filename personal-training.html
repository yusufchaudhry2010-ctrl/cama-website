We offer world-class personal training services led by Sifu Mark Lane — a man with over 40 years experience helping people achieve their health and fitness goals.

Mark has truly "walked the walk" — he was a multiple times World Powerlifting Champion and coached the Irish Powerlifting team. He holds black belts in multiple martial arts disciplines, bringing a uniquely holistic approach to personal training.

Training can be conducted at Mark's home gym in Tylers Green, or he is happy to travel locally to train with you at your location.
