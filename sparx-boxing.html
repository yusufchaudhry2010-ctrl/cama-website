Boxing for Fitness & Fun

"I've done [ANOTHER TYPE OF FITNESS BOXING] before but it was mainly CV not boxing. This was great and also worked the brain. Loved it!"

— SparX Student

We run SparX — Boxing for Fitness classes. These are not martial arts classes but are aimed for fitness and fun in a friendly atmosphere. All levels of ability and fitness are welcome.

SparX combines proper boxing technique with fitness conditioning. You'll learn real punching combinations, footwork and defensive skills — but in a non-competitive, supportive group environment designed to get you fit and have fun doing it.

What to Expect

🥊 Real boxing technique
❤️ Cardio conditioning
🧠 Brain & body workout
🤝 Friendly atmosphere
🏃 All fitness levels
🎯 Pad work & bag work
