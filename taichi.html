Sun & Yang Style Tai Chi

We study both Sun & Yang style of Tai Chi — two of the most widely practised and respected styles in the world. Each brings its own unique qualities: Yang style with its large, expansive movements; Sun style with its agile, compact footwork and flowing transitions.

Taught by Sifu Mark Lane — a practitioner with decades of experience — our Tai Chi programme is suitable for complete beginners through to advanced students. Classes are relaxed, welcoming and suitable for all ages.

Regular Tai Chi practice is known to improve balance, flexibility, cardiovascular health and mental wellbeing. It is truly a practice for life.
